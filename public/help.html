FAQ

Once you have logged in you will find a "create new event" button in the header. Clicking on that will take you to our event creation page

Once you have created your event you can share the unique link in the URL bar of your browser to your friends who can use it to enter their details and participate in the poll!

When you first access an event page you haven't responded to yet. A pop up will appear asking if you would like to sync your Google Calender. Follow the prompts to auto respond.

Once an event is confirmed, you can find a button to add to your Google Calendar on the event page.

Pressing the "Reject Invite" button on the event page will reject the invite.

The settings menu in your dashboard page allows you to turn off email notifications by function.
